What is water footprint?

The water footprint measures the amount of fresh water utilized in the production or supply of goods and services. There are three types of water footprint:
1.Blue:- Water consumed from surface water(lakes and river)and groundwater. Used in our day to day life.
2.Green:- Water consumed from rain water.Mostly used in agricultural industries.
3.Grey:- Water consumed from sources is used to dilute pollutants down to safe concentration.
