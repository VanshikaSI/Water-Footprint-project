"Every drop counts:Know your water footprint,reduce your impact & let sustainability flow."

In areas where water is scarce, people face difficulties in obtaining enough water for drinking, washing, and nourishing crops, which can lead to economic decline. Inadequate sanitation can result in deadly water-borne illnesses. Our project aims to calculate water consumption by families and increase efficiency in water usage. The water footprint measures the amount of fresh water utilized in the production or supply of goods and services. There are three types of water footprint: Blue, Green, and Grey.
